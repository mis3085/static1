Dashcam DrivePro 10

Transcend's DrivePro 10 dashcam features the STARVIS™ image sensor to capture sharp, high-resolution images in superb, rich color even in low light, making it perfect for filming during both day and night. Supporting 1080P Full HD recording at 60fps, the DrivePro 10 captures every crucial detail, such as license plates. It features Wi-Fi connectivity that allows it to work with Transcend’s exclusive DrivePro app. You can easily access video evidence and report it to law enforcement and insurance companies without needing to remove the memory card. In addition, the dashcam utilizes the latest format-free technology to reduce memory card errors, obviating the need for frequent formatting. The DrivePro 10 comes in a compact, discreet design that allows it to be flexibly mounted on your front or rear windshield. Being able to easily hide behind the rearview mirror out of sight, the DrivePro 10 offers the driver an unobstructed view of the road ahead or behind.
